About More Together

After several years of sharing an unbreakable bond as best friends, Tom and Lucy, now in their teenage years, find themselves at a crossroads, embracing the vulnerability of a new chapter in their relationship. The decision to spend a pivotal night together opens the door to a blossoming romance, intertwining their lives in unexpected ways. However, as their connection deepens and the complexities of adulthood loom large, they are confronted with a significant twist of fate.

Lucy's 'time of the month' is unexpectedly delayed, prompting the duo to confront the responsibilities, uncertainties, and challenges that come with the journey into adulthood. This unexpected turn of events becomes a poignant backdrop, forcing Tom and Lucy to navigate the nuances of love, commitment, and the inexorable march of time, as they grapple with the profound implications of their newfound intimacy and the realities of growing up.

About Before They Were Them

In the glittering world of 'Before They Were Them,' an exhilarating short musical, a diverse group of aspiring artists navigates the challenges of pursuing fame. From the vibrant streets to the dazzling stages, the film follows their intertwining journeys, exploring the sacrifices, triumphs, and heartbreaks they encounter on their quest for stardom.

Set against a backdrop of catchy tunes and dynamic choreography, 'Before They Were Them' celebrates the resilience and passion that propel these individuals into the spotlight. As friendships are forged, dreams are tested, and melodies become anthems, the film captures the essence of the transformative power of music on the road to recognition and success.
